Our Innovations

Delta-Wing Design

The Delta-Wing Design ensures that our plane provides space efficiently for freight containers to fit in our plane

Seaplane

Inspired by The PBY Catalina. This allows our plane to taxi out to the ocean to take off and land back on the ocean and taxi back to the port.

Supercritical Airfoil

This airfoil allows our plane to go supersonic without making too much sound

Shifting between Transonic and Supersonic

On flight portions over land, the plane will fly at transonic speeds just under the speed of sound, which will maintain the advantage of high-speed aviation while avoiding issues with noise complaints. Air Traffic Control (ATC) will assist pilots to fly at the correct speed over a given flight region, similar to the current process with flight altitude.

Compatible with Existing Ports

Our product is designed to use existing infrastructure at ports to load and unload freight.
